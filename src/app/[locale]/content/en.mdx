# Welcome to <span className="text-blue-600">4D</span> Engineers

**industrial design** · **firmware** · **electronics** · **mechanics**

4D Engineers is a network of experienced engineers for the development of complex (mechatronic) products. The name 4D Engineers represents the 4 disciplines we have at our disposal: **Design, Electronics, Firmware & Mechanics**.

By having teams in which every discipline is represented, better design choices can be made. Some solutions are primarily mechanical with electronic support, others are more electronics with firmware and simple mechanics. In all developments, the designer continues to look over the shoulder.

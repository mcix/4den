# Our Approach

## Methodology

Perhaps just as important as our people is our development methodology. We use the internally developed **'Hardware Scrum'** method. Key features of this method are the strong mix between thinking and doing, the daily (short) consultation with the client and the 2-week delivery cycles.

We use a number of tools that allow developments to proceed (much) faster and cause less deadline stress. We are happy to tell you more about this methodology.

## Philosophy

We develop, with our client's consent, according to the **QIS philosophy**. This philosophy states that a product should be built with the best available purchase and manufacturing parts, be suitable for easy repair or upgrade, and that the end user should be able to carry out as many modifications as possible themselves.

Our experience is that users of a product or system are happy to carry out repairs or upgrades themselves, as long as it is straightforward. This does not come at the expense of the SLA.
